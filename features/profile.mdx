The Profile page (`/profile`) lets you view your gamification stats, update your account information, change your password, and log out.

## Profile overview

At the top of the page, your avatar displays either your profile picture (if set) or your name's first initial. Below it, your display name and email are shown at a glance.

## Editing your profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Profile">
    Click your avatar or the Profile link in the sidebar (or bottom nav on mobile) to open the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your details">
    The **Editar Perfil** card contains three editable fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Nome** | Your display name (required, minimum 1 character) |
    | **E-mail** | Your login email address |
    | **Foto de Perfil (URL)** | Optional URL to an image used as your avatar |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Salvar**. On success, a toast notification confirms the update and the sidebar/nav refresh to show your new name.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The profile form is pre-filled with your current data. Changes are validated client-side with **Zod** before being sent to the API. The profile is also cached in `useAppCache` — it updates immediately after a successful save without requiring a page reload.
</Note>

## Changing your password

The **Alterar Senha** section is embedded within the same profile card.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Current password">
    The **Senha atual** field is read-only and pre-filled from your session. It is stored temporarily in `sessionStorage` during your active session for convenience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New password">
    Enter your desired new password in the **Nova Senha** field. The password must be at least **6 characters** long.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Salvar**. The password change is only sent to the API if the **Nova Senha** field is non-empty. If you leave it blank, only your profile fields (name, email, picture) are updated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Both the current and new password fields have visibility toggles (eye icons). The current password field is read-only — you cannot type into it manually. If your session has expired, the field will be empty and you will need to re-authenticate.
</Warning>

## Logging out

At the bottom of the page, a **Sair da conta** button clears your authentication state:

- Removes `token` and `user` from `localStorage`
- Removes `_up` from `sessionStorage`
- Resets `useAuthStore` to unauthenticated
- Redirects you to the home page (`/`)

<Tip>
  Logging out does not clear the in-memory app cache (`useAppCache`). If you log back in as a different user, the cache will be stale until the new session's data loads. For a clean slate, the app performs fresh API fetches on each page mount after login.
</Tip>

## UpdateProfileData interface

```typescript
export interface UpdateProfileData {
  name?: string;
  email?: string;
  profile_picture?: string | null;
  currentPassword?: string; // Required when changing password
  newPassword?: string;     // Minimum 6 characters
}
```
