title: "Gamification system"
description: "Earn XP points and maintain streaks to stay motivated and build lasting habits."
---

Mais Hábito uses two core mechanics to keep you engaged: **XP points** you accumulate by completing tasks and challenges, and a **Sequência** (streak) that measures how many consecutive days you've been active.

## UserProfile

All gamification data lives on your user profile:

```typescript
export interface UserProfile {
  id: string;
  name: string;
  email: string;
  profile_picture: string | null;
  points: number;
  current_streak: number;
  max_streak: number;
  created_at: string;
  updated_at: string;
}
```

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| `points` | Total XP points earned across all tasks and challenges |
| `current_streak` | Number of consecutive days with at least one task completion |
| `max_streak` | Your all-time best streak, preserved even after the current streak resets |

## XP points

Every task carries a `points` value you set when creating it. When you complete the task, that value is added to your total `points`. There is no cap — points accumulate indefinitely.

Points are visible in two places:

- **Dashboard** — the "Seus pontos" stat card shows your current total
- **Profile** — your full point history is tied to your account

## Sequência (streak)

The streak is the most visible motivational mechanic in Mais Hábito. It counts how many consecutive calendar days you have completed at least one task.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Current streak" icon="flame">
    `current_streak` increments each day you complete at least one task. It resets to zero if you miss a day.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Max streak" icon="trophy">
    `max_streak` records your all-time best. It only updates when your current streak surpasses it — missing a day does not reduce it.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### How streak days work

The streak is maintained by task completions. Each time you call the complete task endpoint (`POST /task-completions`), the backend checks whether you have completed a task today and updates `current_streak` accordingly.

- First completion of the day — streak advances by one
- Subsequent completions on the same day — streak stays the same (already counted)
- No completion on a given day — streak resets to zero the following day

<Tip>
  You only need to complete **one task** to keep your streak alive for the day. On busy days, mark your lowest-effort daily routine task done first to protect your streak before tackling the rest.
</Tip>

### Where to see your streak

The dashboard shows both `current_streak` (labeled **Sequência**) and `max_streak` (labeled **Max:**) in the stats row. The flame icon next to the card reinforces that this is the streak counter.

## Dashboard stats at a glance

The two stat cards on the dashboard give you an instant read of your gamification standing:

```
┌─────────────────┐  ┌─────────────────┐
│  Seus pontos    │  │  Sequência      │
│  1 240 PTS      │  │  7 Dias         │
│                 │  │  Max: 14        │
└─────────────────┘  └─────────────────┘
```

Both values come from the `UserProfile` fetched on load and cached in the Zustand app store. They refresh silently after every task completion.
